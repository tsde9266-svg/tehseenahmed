About

TAHSEEN AHMED KIANI

Tahseen Ahmed Kiani is a professional photographer based in Gujarkhan, Pakistan, with three years of dedicated studio and field practice at Qaiser Studio. Specialising in wedding, portrait, commercial, and event photography, he brings technical mastery and creative vision to every commission — from intimate family portraits to large-scale ceremonial and corporate productions.

Working with professional camera systems, studio lighting rigs, and on-location setups, Tahseen consistently delivers images of exceptional quality. His post-production expertise in Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop ensures every final image is crafted to the highest professional standard, meeting and exceeding the creative vision of each client.

Based in Gujarkhan and available for commissions across Pakistan.
